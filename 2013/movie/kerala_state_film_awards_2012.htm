kerala state film awards 2012

 

 

Tweet

The Kerala State Film Awards are the most famous film awards for Malayalam movies. These awards are given by “Kerala State Chalachitra Academy” on behalf of the Department of Cultural Affairs, Government of Kerala. An independent jury, which contains the famous personalities from the film field is decided the winner of these awards. This jury is formed by the academy and the Department of Cultural Affairs of Kerala. The academy annually invites films for the award and the jury analyzes the films that are submitted before deciding the winners. These awards are designed to promote the films and encourage artistes and technicians. These awards are declared by the Minister for Cultural Affairs and are presented by the Chief Minister of Kerala.

The 2012 Kerala State Film Awards were announced on 22/2/2012 in Thiruvananthapuram. State Minister for forest,sports and cinema . K.B Ganeshkumar announced the awards at a press meet along with Jury Chairman I V Sasi. A total of 84 films were considered for this awards. Prithviraj got the best actor award in celluloid,and Rima Kallingal got Best actress award in 22 female kottayam.
