Seven movies will compete for the commercial success during the New Year season 2013. The new superstars of Malayalam will make their presence felt through the movies 'Annayum Rasoolum' and 'My Fan Ramu '. Meera Jasmin will have her 'Lisammayude Veedu' for the season while Siddique will have his 'Ente' on theatres. The popular anchor of the TV show Idea Star Singer, Ranjini Haridas, is all set to act in her first film 'Entry'. The young promising actor Sunny Wayne 's 'Ni Ko Na Cha' and Ram Charan's 'Hai Ramcharan ' will also grace the theaters for this New Year.
