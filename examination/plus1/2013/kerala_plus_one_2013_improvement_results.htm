Kerala plus two certificate distribution

The certificates of kerala plus two examinations will be distributed by the second week of June 2013.The certificates,downloaded from the websites can be attested and provisional certificates can also be obtained from the Higher Secondary Directorates.

Kerala Plus Two grace marks

All Plus 2 and VHSE NCC cadets who have 75% attendance wiil get 5% Grace marks for Kerala plus 2 examination 2013.Apply soon through concerned units. For more details contact the nearest NCC unit or www.keralancc.org.

kerala plus two 2013 revaluation

The last date for submitting the application is on or before 29/05/2013.It is the time to apply for revaluation,photostat of answer papers or scrutiny of papers. The double valuated subjects,Physics,Chemistry and Mathematics will not be under taken for revaluation or scrutiny, but only you can get the photostat copy of these subjects.This can be applied at the concerned schools. The application form will be available at the schools and also in hss portals. The application must be submitted within 15 days after recieving the marklist.The The fee for revaluation is Rs 400/- per paper,Rs75 Scrutiny and Rs 300 for the photocopy of answers. After the due date the photocopy can be obtained for Rs 1000/- upto 2013 March and Rs 2000/- from 2013 April-2014 March 31. Head of account for challan fee is '0202-01-102-97(3) Other reciepts'. The students from Gulf countries and Lakshadweep should send fee as d/d in favour of Director of HSE.

In revaluation the increased score of 5% maximum score will not be considered. But if it leads to 100% score or helps to pass with or without moderation or eligiblity for professional course may be considered. The less score in revaluation will not affect the previous results or scores. If the difference in the score in revaluation is more than 10% it is valuated once again and the average score of both will be taken as the results.And if it is also 10% or above it is valuated for a third time and the average of nearest two scores will be taken as final result.
If there arises an increase of 10% or above in the revaluation score, it is sure that necessary disciplinary actions will be taken against the concerned first evaluators.
This is also to be noted that the students apply for revaluation has the chance to check the revaluated paper within the time period allotted by Secretary of Examination Board. And those who apply for scrutiny can also check whether any answer is not evaluated or any further errors in the evaluation of any answers.
