kerals plus 2 single window admission 2012

All students of SSLC-state CBSC,ICSC& THSLC, equivalent examination of other state countries should pass minimum D+ grade for each papper.for other state&countries the students should have minimum pass as per this board rule.
Age limit- complete 15yrs as on 1 june 2012& not exceed 20yrs.students for kerala public examination had no minimum&maximum age limit. for other exemption for SC/ST 2yrs of exemption for high age limit for IED-bliend,deaf or mentally retiered high age limit in 25yrs.
Cost of application form Rs 10/-.The application form is available at all Government and Aided schools in every district. There are separate application forms and prospectus for each revenue district. And so the student who seeks admission in the schools in a revenue district should obtain the application form and prospectus from any of the schools in that revenue district and submit the filled in application in any of the schools in the same district. The prospectus obtained from one district will contain the school details of that district only. Those who seek admission in more than one revenue district should submit separate applications for each district. For Single Window Admission the students should not submit more than one application in a district. After submission of the application form in the school the students should obtain the acknowledgement slip duly signed by the Principal and affixed with the school seal. The students should keep this acknowledgement slip with them till they get permanent admission. The slip is essential for all further enquiries on the application. Those who passed kerala sslc 2012 are allowed to submit the internet print out of the mark list along with the application. Applicants need not submit separate certificates in proof of caste, religion, local body, taluk etc. if these details are there in SSLC. However the candidates should submit self attested copies of relevant certificates if they claim benefits the proof of which is not there in SSLC. For checking admission details and allotments : www.hscap.kerala.gov.in
