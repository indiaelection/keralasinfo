kerala plus two say and improvement examinations

Kerala plus two say and improvement examinations will be held from 18 June to 22 June 2012. The regular students who appeared first time in plus exam in March can write SAY examination for all the failed subjects. If you got eligible for higher studies in all subjects,you have got the chance to write the improvement exam for the improving the score of any oneof the subjects. The say and improvement examinations are allowed only for second year theory papers. Those who missed to attend practical exams can apply for practical exams. The last date for the application is May 24.
