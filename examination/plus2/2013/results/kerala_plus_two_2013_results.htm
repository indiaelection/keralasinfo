kerala plus two 2013 revaluation

Kerala Plus Two 2013 students have got the chance to apply for Revaluation and scrutiny for the papers, double valuation sactioned,but only for theory papers. The circular willbe released along with results. The application must be submitted within 15 days after recieving the marklist.The The fee for revaluation is Rs 400/- per paper,Rs75 Scrutiny and Rs 300 for the photocopy of answers. After the due date the photocopy can be obtained for Rs 1000/- upto 2013 March and Rs 2000/- from 2013 April-2014 March 31. Head of account for challan fee is '0202-01-102-97(3) Other reciepts'. The students from Gulf countries and Lakshadweep should send fee as d/d in favour of Director of HSE.

In revaluation the increased score of 5% maximum score will not be considered. But if it leads to 100% score or helps to pass with or without moderation or eligiblity for professional course may be considered. The less score in revaluation will not affect the previous results or scores. If the difference in the score in revaluation is more than 10% it is valuated once again and the average score of both will be taken as the results.And if it is also 10% or above it is valuated for a third time and the average of nearest two scores will be taken as final result.
If there arises an increase of 10% or above in the revaluation score, it is sure that necessary disciplinary actions will be taken against the concerned first evaluators.
This is also to be noted that the students apply for revaluation has the chance to check the revaluated paper within the time period allotted by Secretary of Examination Board. And those who apply for scrutiny can also check whether any answer is not evaluated or any further errors in the evaluation of any answers.
