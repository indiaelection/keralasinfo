Kerala Higher Secondary Education Say-Improvement examinations will be conducted from 24.5.2013 to 31.05.2013. The regular students who appeared first time in plus two exam in March can write SAY examination for all the failed subjects. If you got eligible for higher studies in all subjects,you have got the chance to write the improvement exam for the improving the score of any one of the subjects. The say and improvement examinations are allowed only for second year theory papers. Those who missed to attend practical exams can apply for practical exams. The last date for the application is May 15.
