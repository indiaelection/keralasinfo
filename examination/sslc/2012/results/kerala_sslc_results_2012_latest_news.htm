Kerala SSLC grace marks

All SSLC students in NCC at school level who have 75% attendance wiil get 5% Grace marks for SSLC examination 2012. For application and more details contact the nearest NCC unit or www.keralancc.org.

caste/religion for kerala sslc 2012

There is no compulsion for filling the caste/religion column in kerala sslc 2012 certificate. Those who fill the caste/religion with 'Nil' will not get any concession for the caste/religion.
