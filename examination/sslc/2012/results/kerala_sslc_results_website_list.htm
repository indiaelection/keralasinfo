kerala sslc say examination 2012

Kerala sslc say exam 2012 will be conducted within two weeks just after the declaration of the sslc exam going on now. It is to be noted that those who prepared for the exam and could not attend the examination due to accident,contageous desease snd death of parents or brothers and sisters will have the chance to appear for all the missed papers for SAY examination 2012.The regular students who could not attend any two papers in the sslc examination for any critical situations also get the chance to attend SAY examination 2012. But one thing to note that those who could write all the papers in this SSLC examination will be allowed to write only two papers in the SAY examination 2012.And those who are not elegible for higher studies for two subjects can get the elegiblity by appearing SAY examination.
