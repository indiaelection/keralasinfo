  kerala sslc results 2013

 

The Deputy Director Sri M D Murali informed that Kerala sslc results 2013 will be published on 24/04/2013 after 11.30 AM Wednesday . This is the first time the result is publishing so earlier.This year SSLC valuation had completed in the first week of April itself. And so the result is publishing this much earlier.

kerala sslc aptitude test 2013

The kerala education department introduces online aptitude test for all kerala sslc students.The state institute of Education Technology has been getting ready to conduct this test.The main objective of this test is to provide proper guidance for higher studies by evaluating scientifically the student's inborn capabilities and aptitude,learning skill, language skill,memory power,imagination, thought,reasoning power, personality. The results are to be reported online. There is no compulsion for this test. The test fee is Rs 100/- and the timing The test can be written in English or Malayalam.
