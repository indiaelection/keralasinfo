  kerala SSLC say examination 2013-tips

Regular student who missed any two papers in sslc examination march 2013 under any critic situation get the chance to write sslc say examination 2013.
If the students registered for sslc examination 2013 could not attend the examination or could not able to complete the examination, they are allowewd to write more than two papers in sslc say examination 2013.
Those who could not get atleast D+ grade for any two papers are deserved to write say examination 2013.
For IT, the theory examination only can be improved through say examination.
Only the scores in written examination in terminal evaluation (TE) can be improved through say examination.
No revaluation is allowed for say examination.

The sslc say examination 2013 will be held in selected centers around the district.the student should apply for say examination at the centres he/she had attend the sslc examination march 2013 .
Students from gulf schools can apply for write say examination 2013 at any exam centre in kerala.
The computer print out of sslc exam march 2013 is to apply for say.
The exam fees is RS.100/- per paper.
