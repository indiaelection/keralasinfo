The State Council Educational Research and Training (SCERT), Kerala is going to conduct TET Exam for the recruitment of teachers in lower primary,upper primary,and high school level.

The provisions will become applicable from the Accademic year 2012-2013 . This is note worthy that these rules have been implimented to the provisions of the Right of the children to free and Compulsary Education (RTE) Act 2012.

The conduct of the examination is entrusted with Pareeksha Bhavan, Kerala. A prospectus for the examination is made available in the website of Pareeksha Bhavan (www.keralapareekshabhavan.in).

Candidates are strictly advised to go through the same before submitting the application form. Qualifications for applying for each Category is given in detail in the Prospectus.

Applications are invited for Kerala Teachers Eligibility Test (KTET) for Teachers at various levels as per the detail provided:

  • category I : Teachers for Lower Primary
  • category II : Teachers for Upper primary
  • category III : Teachers for High school
