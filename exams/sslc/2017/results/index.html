The Kerala Secondary School Leaving Certificate (commonly referred to as SSLC) is a certification obtained by a student on successful completion of an examination at the end of study at the secondary schooling level in Kerala, India. The SSLC is obtained on passing the grade 10 public examination commonly referred to as 'class 10 board examinations' in Kerala.
