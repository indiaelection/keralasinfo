kerala grand shopping festival 2011

here is all detailed news of kerala grand shopping festival 2011

                 

 

ഗ്രാന്‍ഡ് കേരള ഷോപ്പിങ് ഫെസ്റ്റിവല്‍ സീസണ്‍ 5 ഡിസംബര്‍ 1ന

Yet another exciting and fun-filled shopping festival is round the corner. The Grand Kerala Shopping Festival (GKSF) - the signature shopping festival of Kerala would soon unveil its irresistible charm for the fifth time in God's Own Country.A Government of Kerala initiative, GKSF made its debut in the year 2007 and went on to attain the stature of a much-awaited annual event. Conducted by the Department of Tourism in association with the departments of Industries & Commerce, Finance and Local Self Government, over the years the festival has become a successful exercise in the process of transforming Kerala into a hub for international shopping experienceFresh enterprises, mind blowing offers and exciting prizes; both for shops and shoppers, easily make GKSF the biggest ever shopping extravaganza. For emigrant Keralites, this season brings another fabulous offer. They will be exempted from the Value Added Tax as GKSF will be taking care of it.added offers, added discounts and added satisfaction and fun awaits the shopping community of kerala in this year's edition of the grand kerala shopping festival.
