Nehru Trophy Boat Race 2016

The Nehru Trophy Boat Race on the Punnamda Lake, in Alappuzha,Kerala,India. held on the second Saturday 13th of August 2016, nehru trophy boat race 2016 is a popular Vallam Kali held in the Punnamada Lake near Alappuzha, Kerala, India. Vallam Kali or Vallamkali literally means boat play/game, but can be translated to boat race in English. The most popular event of the race is the competition of Chundan Vallams (snake boats).
