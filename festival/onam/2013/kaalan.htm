Onam recipe kaalan

One of the highlight of Onam is Onam Sadhya, the traditional food feast. The recipe includes rice, fried vegetables, avial (vegeble mix), sambar, rasam, pickles, payasam (a milk sweet), puttu (a rice flour sweet), babana halwa, dry fruit kesari, papad, idli, masala dosas, coconut chutney, etc., all served traditionaly in babana leaves.The two famous recipes for onam are Olan and Aviyal.

KAALAN

Onam Kaalan Ingredients

Raw plantian(nendra kaya) -1
Yam(chena) -1 cup(chopped)
Curd -2 cup
Grated coconut -1/2 of 1
Pepper-5
Green chilly-1
Red chill-1/2tsp
Turmeric powder-1/2tsp
Cumin seeds-1/4tsp
Salt-to taste
Curry leaves-1 string
Mustard seeds-1/4tsp
Fenugreek seeds(uluva)-10
Dry red chilly-2 (chopped)
Curry leaves-1 string>
Oil-2tsp

Onam Kaalan Method

Peel and cube the plantian and yam.In a kadai add plantain and yam pieces along with turmeric powder,chilly powder ,salt ,curry leaves and 1 cup of water.Cook till the vegetables are done.Side by side grind coconut ,pepper,cumin seeds and green chilly to a fine paste. Add this paste to the cooked vegetables. Boil them well.Stirring continuously.Add salt.Finally add curd to this and simmer for2-3 minutes..Don`t boil.Season with mustard seeds,red chilly ,fenugreek seeds and curry leaves.Add this to the gravy.The simple and tasty Kaalan is ready to serve.
