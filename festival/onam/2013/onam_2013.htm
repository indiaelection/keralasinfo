Onam 2013 celebrations commence with Atham on August 21 and ends with Thiruvonam on August 29.Onam2013 celebrations last for ten days as the first day is on August 21 Tuesday-Atham,the second day is on August 22 Wednesday - Chithira,the third day is on August 23 Thursday-Chothy, the fourth day is on August 24 Friday - Vishakham,the fifth day is on August 25 Saturday - Anizham,the sixth day is on August 26 Sunday -Thrikketta,the seventh day is on August 27 Monday-Moolam, the eighth and ninth day is on August 28 Tuesday -pooradam and uthradam and the tenth day is on August 29 Wednesday Thiruvonam.
