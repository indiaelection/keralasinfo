One of the highlight of Onam is Onam Sadhya, the traditional food feast. The recipe includes rice, fried vegetables, avial (vegeble mix), sambar, rasam, pickles, payasam (a milk sweet), puttu (a rice flour sweet), babana halwa, dry fruit kesari, papad, idli, masala dosas, coconut chutney, etc., all served traditionaly in babana leaves.The two famous recipes for onam are Olan and Aviyal.

MABAZHA PULISSERRY

Ingredients:

Ripe Mango-10 no(u will get small mangoes specially for this).. Otherwise use some other ripe mango and cut into small pieces
Green chilly-2 (make fine paste)
Turmeric powder-1/4tsp
Sugar-1tsp
Chilly powder-one pinch
Curry leaves-1 string
Grated coconut-1/2 of 1
Garlic-1/2
Curd-2cup
Water-1cup,

Seasoning:

Dry red chilly-3
Fenugreek seeds-1/4tsp
Mustard seeds-1/2 tsp
Curry leaves-few

Method:

Peel the mangoes and cook them with green chilly paste, turmeric powder,chilly powder,salt and curry leaves.Add water up to the top of the mangoes.Cook till the mangoes get soft.Take a blender grind grated coconut,garlic , curry leaves(3),and handful of water and make a smooth fine pasteâ€¦or if u have an ammikkallu then its great. Add this paste to the cooked mambhazham and mix well. Add salt as per ur taste.On a low heat allow it to boil. Beat curd with a spoon.Now add curd and mix well with the mangoes..Bring it to boil for one min.Add sugar also. Do not allow the yogurt to curdle.Then take it from the fire and keep aside.Heat coconut oil in a pan.Add mustard seeds and when splutter add fenugreek seeds , dry red chilly and curry leaves.Add this seasoning to the pulissery.you are tasty and simple mambazha pulissery is ready to serve.
