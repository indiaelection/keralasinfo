Onam 2016 date

Onam 2016 comes in September 13, 2016 (Tuesday) . The celebrations of Onam start on Atham day, 10 days before Thiruvonam. The 10 days are part of the traditional Onam celebrations and each day has its own importance in various rituals and traditions.
