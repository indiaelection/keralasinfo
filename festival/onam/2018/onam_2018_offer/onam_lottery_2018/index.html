Onam lottery 2018

Kerala State Lotteries is a lottery programme run by the Government of Kerala. Established in 1967, under the lottery department of the Government of Kerala, it is the first of its kind in India. The department soon became a model for other states in India. Soon many other states jumped into the bandwagon by starting their own lotteries.
