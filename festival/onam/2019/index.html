Onam 2019

Onam is the biggest festival in the South Indian state of Kerala. It is also the state festival of Kerala with State holidays on 4 days starting from Onam Eve (Uthradom) to the 3rd Onam Day. It is the harvest festival in kerala and celebrated by all the malayalee natives all around the world.Onam is celebrated in the Malayalam month Chingam.
