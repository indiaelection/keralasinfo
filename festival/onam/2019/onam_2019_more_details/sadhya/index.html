Onam sadhya 2019

One of the highlight of onam is onam sadhya 2019, the traditional food feast. the recipe includes rice, fried vegetables, avial (vegeble mix), sambar, rasam, pickles, payasam (a milk sweet), puttu (a rice flour sweet), babana halwa, dry fruit kesari, papad, idli, masala dosas, coconut chutney, etc., all served traditionaly in babana leaves.the two famous recipes for onam are olan and aviyal.Onam is the biggest festival in the South Indian state of Kerala. It is also the state festival of Kerala with State holidays on 4 days starting from Onam Eve (Uthradom) to the 3rd Onam Day. It is the harvest festival in kerala and celebrated by all the malayalee natives all around the world.Onam is celebrated in the Malayalam month Chingam.
