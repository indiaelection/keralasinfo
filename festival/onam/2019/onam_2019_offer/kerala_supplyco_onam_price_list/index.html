Onam 2019 fair price list

Kerala Government to organise 9000 special markets for Onam 2019 to ensure availability of essential commodities at fair prices. Supply Co: 1662 ,Consumerfed :3500 ,agricultural department:2000 , and Kudumbasree 1052 stalls.Besides free Onam gift kits to be distributed to 6 lakh families. Supplyco will start several Onam bazaars and Onam fairs during this Onam 2019 season. The metro cities will have Onam Metro fairs, along with town fairs, taluk fairs etc. A total of 1300 Onam special Supplyco bazaars will be available in Kerala during Onam 2019 season from 01.09.2019 (Sunday) to 10.09.2019 (Tuesday).
