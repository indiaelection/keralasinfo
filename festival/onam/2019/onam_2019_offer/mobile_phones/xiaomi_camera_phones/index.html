Onam exam in kerala 2019

Onam kerala school (First Terminal-quarterly) examination starting from 26.08.2019(Monday) to 05.09.2019(Thursday) for classes 9 to 12, Lower Primary (LP) school examination starting from 31.08.2019(Saturday) to 05.09.2019(Thursday) in morning for classes 1 to 4, Upper Primary (UP) school examination starting from 31.08.2019(Saturday) to 05.09.2019(Thursday) in evening for classes 5 to 8 in Kerala schools. Last year the Onam 2018 examination could not be held on time due to kerala floods 2018. Many schools in the state had doubled up as flood relief camps
