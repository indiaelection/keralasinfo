Thripunithura Athachamayam 2019

Thripunithura Athachamayam: Starting Day Of Onam 2019 in Kerala, Thripunithura Athachamayam Conducted every year on the Atham asterism of the Malayalam month Chingam (Leo), the event held at the temple town of Thripunithura in Ernakulam districts is a celebration of a legendary victory of the Raja (King) of Kochi. In olden days it was customary for the king to travel with his entire entourage to the Thripunithura fort. now without the king, still retains its majestic charm, and is conducted in a spectacular manner Onam is the biggest festival in the South Indian state of Kerala. It is also the state festival of Kerala with State holidays on 4 days starting from Onam Eve (Uthradom) to the 3rd Onam Day. It is the harvest festival in kerala and celebrated by all the malayalee natives all around the world.Onam is celebrated in the Malayalam month Chingam.
