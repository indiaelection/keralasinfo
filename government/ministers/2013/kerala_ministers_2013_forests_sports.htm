Profile

Keezhoote Balakrishna Pillai Ganesh Kumar (born 25 May 1966) is an Indian film and TV actor and politician, now serving as Minister for Forests & Environment, Sports and Cinema in the Government of Kerala. He was elected to the State Legislative Assembly from Pathanapuram in Kollam district for the first time in 2001 and has represented the constituency ever since

Son of Shri. R. Balakrishna Pillai and Smt. Valsala Balakrishna Pillai; born at Kottarakkara on 25th May 1966; B.Com (course completed); Cine Artist; Social Worker
