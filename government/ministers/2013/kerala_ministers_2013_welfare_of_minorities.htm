Profile

Manjalamkuzhi Ali is an Indian politician and minister in the Government of Kerala. He was elected to Kerala Legislative Assembly from Perinthalmanna constituency in 2011.

Son of Shri. Mohamed and Smt. Aysha; born at Panangangara on Ist January 1952; Intermediate; Businessman.

Was Chairman, Mahakavi Moyinkutty Vaidyar Smaraka Trust (1996-2001); Director, KSFDC (1996-01); Director, NORKA-ROOTS (2006-11).

Wife : Smt. Raziya
Children : Two sons and two daughters
