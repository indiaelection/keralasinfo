Profile

Shri.K.P.Mohanan, Born at Puthoor near Panoor in Kannur district of Kerala on 3 March 1950. Son of former Minister Shri.P.R.Kurup and Late Smt. K.P. Leelavathy Amma, is elected from Koothuparambu Assembly Constituency in the 2011 Assembly Elections representing Janata Dal Secular (JDS). He was previously elected from Peringalam Assembly Constituency in 2001 and 2006.

Today, he is the Chairman of the Socialist Janata State Parliamentary Board and Member of its State Committee. He is elected from Kuthuparamba Constituency to the Kerala Legislative Assembly representing Socialist Janata(D). He is becoming Minister for the first time.

Wife : Smt. Hemaja
