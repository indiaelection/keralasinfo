Profile

V. S. Sivakumar is an Indian politician and the present Minister for Health, Family Welfare and Devaswom, Government of Kerala.

V.S Siva Kumar was born to Shri K.V. Sadasivan Nair and Smt.B. Subhadra Amma at Amaravila near Neyyattinkara on 30th May 1960.

He was Member of Parliament in the 13th Lok Sabha representing Thiruvananthapuram Parliamentary Constituency. (Member, Parliamentary Standing Committee for Science, Technology and Commerce.Member, Consultative Committee for Ministry of Road Transport & Highways). He has served as DCC President, AICC member, KPCC member and general secretary.He is elected from Thiruvananthapuram Constituency to the Kerala Legislative Assembly representing Indian National Congress. He is becoming a Minister for the first time.
