THIRUVANANTHAPURAM:: Former Kerala minister M P Gangadharan died at a private hospital here on Monday. He was 77. He will be cremated at Shanthi Kavadom in Thycaud at 5 pm on Tuesday. His body will be kept for public homage at the KPCC headquarters before that. Gangadharan had been admitted to a private hospital for the past few weeks and was undergoing treatment for multiple ailments. He served as the irrigation minister in the 1982-87 K Karunakaran ministry, but could not complete his term as he was forced to resign in 1986 following a controversy over the marriage of his daughter. It was alleged that his daughter was not 18 at the time of her marriage.
