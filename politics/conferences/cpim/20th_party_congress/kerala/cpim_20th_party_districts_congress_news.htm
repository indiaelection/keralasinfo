cpim 20th party districts congress news

The CPM party central committee meet held in Hyderabad has decided to convene the 20th party Congress in Kerala. The party congress will be on the moth of April.

The party has been considering Kozhikode, Kannur, Kollam and Thiruvananthapuram as the venue for Congress . The state committee would inform in which district the programme would be held, last time party congress was held in Kerala in 1988 and the venue was Thiruvananathapuram.
