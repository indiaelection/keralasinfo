Cpim Kerala state conference 2018


The conference, being held ahead of the 22nd party congress, began with veteran leader V S Achuthanandan hoisting the flag at the venue for representatives’ conference earlier the day. Ahead of the 22nd Party Congress in Hyderabad in April 2018, the CPM will hold its state conference in Thrissur from February 22-25, 2018. The delegate session will be attended by 566 delegates (475 elected delegates, 87 state committee members, 4 state committee invitees) and 16 observers representing the Party units across all 14 districts. 31,700 branch conferences, 2,093 local committee conferences, 206 area conferences and 14 district conferences had been held according to schedule. Thrissur Cpim state conference 2018 also decided to build 2,000 new houses for the poor and down trodden.

Cpim Kerala state conference 2018 program

Delegate Conference : Regional Theatre (V V Dakshinamurthi Nagar) from 22nd February, 10 am - 25th February 1 pm. Delegate conferences will begin 10 am on 22nd February. Shri. V.S. Achudanandan will do the flag hoisting for Delegate meetings and Shri. Sitaram Yechuri will inaugurate the delegate conference. CPI-M politburo member Kodiyeri Balakrishnan on Sunday was unanimously elected as the party's state secretary for a second three-year term.
