Rakesh Singha (1956-) is an Indian politician and member of the Communist Party of India (Marxist). Rakesh Singha is a current member of legislative assembly (MLA) in Himachal Pradesh Legislative Assembly (elected in 2017) from the Theog Constituency. From 1993 to 1996, he was a member of legislative assembly (MLA) from Shimla constituency in Shimla district.Adrees:-Vill. Mangsu, P.O.Thanedhar, Tehsil Kumarsain, District Shimla,H.P. Rakesh Singha is a Member of the Central Committee of the CPI(M). rakeshsinghacpim@gmail.com
