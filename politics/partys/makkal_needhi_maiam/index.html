Makkal Needhi Maiam MNM (English: People's Justice Forum) (Tamil: மக்கள் நீதி மய்யம் ) is a regional political party formed by Tamil film actor Kamal Haasan in the Indian state of Tamil Nadu,

Kamal Haasan

Kamal Haasan (born 7 November 1954) is an Indian actor, politician, director, screenwriter, producer, playback singer and lyricist who works primarily in Tamil cinema. Kamal has won awards including three National Film Awards, the second-most by any Indian actor, and nineteen Filmfare Awards. His production company, Rajkamal International, has produced several of his films.
