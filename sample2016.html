Kerala Ministers 2016

Pinarayi Vijayan Kerala's 22nd chief minister

Pinarayi Vijayan would be sworn-in as Kerala's 22nd chief minister on 25.05.2016, in the state capital Thiruvananthapuram. The grand oath taking ceremony was at the central stadium at 4 pm.

CPIM has 12 ministers including Chief Minister Pinarayi Vijayan. While CPI has four ministers, NCP, Congress (S), JD (S) will have one minister each in Kerala new ministers list
