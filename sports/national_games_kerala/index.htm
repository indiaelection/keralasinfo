35th national games kerala government news

Kerala hosted the 27th National Games in 1987and is the proud host for the forthcoming 35th National Games scheduled for 12.12.2012.Government of Kerala and Kerala Olympic Association requested the Indian Olympic Association to permit them to host the National Games in Kerala in response to application dated 15th March 2008. Based on this, the host city contract was signed among GOK, KOA & IOA on 7th November 2008. Subsequently, the National Games Organizing Committee was constituted on 10-12-2008 and the present National Games Secretariat was set up vide G O (MS) No. 101/09/G.Edn dated 23-04-2009 and registered under Travancore Cochin Charitable Societies Registration Act.

The Games is expected to be held at the end of year 2012 in seven districts across the State of Kerala namely Thiruvananthapuram, Kollam, Alappuzha, Kochi, Thrissur, Kozhikode and Kannur. There will be 36 medal events and two demonstration events.
