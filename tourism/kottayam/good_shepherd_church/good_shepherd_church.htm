good shepherd church

The Good Shepherd church is located behind the Civil Station at Kottayam. The Good Shepherd Church is eminent as the first church under the important Diocese of Vijayapuram. The architecture of the church has been largely influenced by Italian designs and patterns. Visitors are overwhelmed with the beauty and grandeur of the church.
